Recovery Pathway

A Clearer Journey From Hospital to Home

One of Nyumbani’s biggest advantages can be showing families exactly how care starts, what happens on day one, and how support continues after discharge.

Before Discharge

Review discharge notes, medications, care risks, and equipment needs before the client gets home.

Day One Setup

Nurse assessment, vitals baseline, medication reconciliation, safety review, and equipment setup if needed.

Recovery Support

Scheduled caregiver visits, nurse follow-ups, and practical goals focused on safer recovery and avoiding readmission.

Family Updates

Keep loved ones informed after each shift or visit with concise updates on care delivered, changes noticed, and next steps.
