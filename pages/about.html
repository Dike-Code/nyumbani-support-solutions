About Nyumbani Support Solutions

Born from a belief that every Kenyan deserves dignified, professional care in the place they call home.

Serving Kenya Since 2020
Our Mission

Nyumbani — "Home" in Swahili

The name says everything. Nyumbani means "home" — and home is where people heal best, age most happily, and feel most themselves.

We founded Nyumbani Support Solutions within Legacy Care Africa's healthcare portfolio after seeing how many Kenyan families struggled to find trustworthy, professional home care. Hospital stays were too short. Nursing homes too institutional. And family caregivers were burning out trying to do it all.

Our answer was to build a service that combines clinical excellence with genuine compassion — one that treats each client as a person with a full life, not a medical condition to manage.

Dignity First

We care for people the way we'd want our own parents cared for.

Clinical Excellence

High standards, continuous training, and evidence-based practices.

Family Partnership

We keep families informed and involved at every stage.

Community Impact

Employing skilled Kenyan caregivers and building local capacity.

Our Parent Organisation

Part of Legacy Care Africa

Nyumbani Support Solutions operates within Legacy Care Africa's broader mission to transform healthcare access across the continent.

Legacy Care Africa Our parent organisation is building a network of healthcare services across East Africa — from community clinics to specialised home care. Nyumbani sits within its home care and support division, Afya Nyumbani Solutions.
Afya Nyumbani Solutions As the home care and wellness arm of Legacy Care Africa, Afya Nyumbani Solutions provides the infrastructure, clinical governance, and training standards that make Nyumbani Support Solutions possible.
