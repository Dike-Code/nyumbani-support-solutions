How We Select Our Team

Less than 20% of applicants make it through our selection process. Here's why that matters to you.

Professional Qualifications

We accept only registered nurses, certified nursing assistants, and trained caregivers with verifiable credentials.

Criminal Background Check

Every caregiver undergoes a thorough DBS check through the Directorate of Criminal Investigations before placement.

Care Training Programme

All caregivers complete our 40-hour induction covering person-centred care, safeguarding, infection control, dementia awareness, first aid, and CPR training.

Reference Verification

We personally contact and verify references from previous employers or supervisors before any hire is confirmed.
