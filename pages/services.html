Our Care Services

Tailored home care solutions for every stage of life — from a few hours a week to intensive daily support.

Most Popular

Elderly Home Care

Our most requested service — helping older adults live safely, comfortably and with full dignity in their own home. We match each client with a caregiver who fits their personality and preferences.

  • Personal hygiene assistance — bathing, dressing, grooming
  • Meal preparation and feeding support
  • Medication reminders and organisation
  • Light housekeeping and laundry
  • Mobility assistance and fall prevention
  • Errand running and appointment escort
  • Regular family progress reports

Clinical Care

Skilled Nursing at Home

Our Nursing Council of Kenya (NCK) registered nurses bring hospital-level clinical care to your home — eliminating the need for repeated clinic visits.

  • Wound assessment, cleaning and dressing changes
  • IV therapy and medication administration
  • Catheter insertion and care
  • Nasogastric tube insertion and feeding
  • Vital signs monitoring (BP, glucose, O₂)
  • Post-surgical care and stoma management
  • Liaison with the treating physician

Social Wellbeing

Companion Care

Loneliness is a health risk. Our companion caregivers provide meaningful company, stimulating activity, and emotional warmth for seniors living alone.

Conversation & Engagement

Meaningful conversation, reading aloud, reminiscence activities, puzzles, and games to keep the mind sharp.

Errands & Outings

Shopping, banking, parks, religious services — we escort seniors out so they remain active in their community.

Safety Oversight

Checking on the home environment, responding to falls, and calling emergency services when needed.

Respite for Families

Relief for family caregivers — a few hours or full days of trusted professional coverage so you can rest.

Recovery Support

Post-Hospital Recovery

The 30 days after discharge are the highest-risk period for readmission. Our post-hospital service bridges the gap between hospital and full independence.

Discharge Planning

We handle the discharge process so you can focus on your loved one, not the stress.

Medical Transport

We provide safe hospital-to-home transport, with ambulance and nursing support when needed

Home Recovery Support

We provide trusted in-home support to help patients recover safely and comfortably after discharge.

Rent, Don't Buy

Medical Equipment Rental

Hospital‑grade equipment delivered directly to your home anywhere in Kenya, professionally set up by our technicians, fully sanitised between each use, and collected when no longer needed — with no large upfront purchase required.

  • Adjustable electric hospital beds
  • Wheelchairs: standard, lightweight, reclining, and foldable electric
  • Oxygen concentrators and portable oxygen cylinders
  • IV drip stands and infusion pumps
  • Walking frames, crutches, and rollators
  • Pressure-relief mattresses and cushions
  • Commode and shower chairs
  • Blood pressure monitors, pulse oximeters, and glucometers

How It Works

Equipment in 3 Simple Steps

1. Call or Request Online

Tell us which equipment you need, for how long, and your location. We'll confirm availability and pricing within 2 hours.

2. We Deliver & Set Up

Our team delivers to your door, assembles and configures the equipment, and demonstrates safe use for the patient and family.

3. We Collect When Done

When care ends or the equipment is no longer needed, we collect, sanitise, and service everything — no hassle for you.
